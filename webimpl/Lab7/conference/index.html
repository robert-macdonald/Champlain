The World Wide Web Conference is an annual conference sponsored by McGill University and the Institut de Technologie de Montreal. This year the conference is being held on October 15-16, 2017 at the Palais des congres in Montreal.

Conference Session are lecture, panel discussion, or workshop format. All sessions are 75 minutes - 60 minutes for presentation and 15 minutes for questions.

Network with Colleagues

We encourage you to submit a proposal via email. Download the form and email it to proposals@conference.com. The proposal deadline is June 10, 2017.

Experience professionals are invited to submit proposals for conference sessions. The conference committee accepts proposals based on topic and information quality. We encourage session on advanced topics, especially innovation Web technology.
